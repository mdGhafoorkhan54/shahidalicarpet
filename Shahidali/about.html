About Shahid Ali Carpet and Furnishing: Weaving a Legacy of Comfort and Style in Abu Dhabi for Over 50 Years For over half a century, Shahid Ali Carpet and Furnishing has been a cornerstone of home décor in Abu Dhabi, transforming houses into homes with our exquisite collection of carpets, sofas, and furnishings. Our enduring legacy is built on a foundation of deep-rooted expertise, an unwavering commitment to quality, and a passion for bringing beauty and comfort to every living space. Under the expert guidance of our proprietor, Saheb Jan, our establishment has become synonymous with trust and excellence in the furnishing industry. His vision and profound knowledge of textiles and design have been the driving force behind our five decades of success. We take pride in offering a diverse and extensive selection of carpets to suit every taste and interior design theme. Our collection is a testament to the rich heritage of carpet weaving from around the world, featuring: Handmade Afghani and Irani Carpets: Each piece tells a story, intricately hand-knotted by skilled artisans who have passed down their craft through generations. These carpets are not merely floor coverings but timeless works of art, celebrated for their rich colours, complex patterns, and exceptional durability. Belgian and Turkish Carpets: Renowned for their sophisticated designs and superior craftsmanship, our Belgian and Turkish carpets bring a touch of European elegance to any room. From classic motifs to contemporary patterns, these carpets are a popular choice for their quality and style. Machine-Made Carpets: Offering a blend of affordability and durability, our machine-made carpets are available in a vast array of designs and colours, perfect for high-traffic areas and modern living. Whether you are decorating a new home or revitalizing a cherished space, Shahid Ali Carpet and Furnishing is your trusted partner. Our extensive range extends beyond carpets to include a curated selection of sofas and other furnishings to complete your interior vision. We are dedicated to providing a seamless and convenient customer experience. To this end, we offer: Free Same-Day Delivery: Your new purchase will be at your doorstep on the same day you order, ensuring you can enjoy your new décor without delay. Professional Installation: Our experienced team will handle the installation of your carpets and furnishings with precision and care. Abu Dhabi-Wide Service: We are pleased to offer our delivery and installation services across the entirety of Abu Dhabi. At Shahid Ali Carpet and Furnishing, we are more than just a business; we are a family with a time-honoured tradition of helping you create a home that is both beautiful and comfortable. We invite you to visit our showroom and experience the legacy of quality and style that has defined us for over 50 years.
